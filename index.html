our services

free checkups

Free checkups provide an opportunity for healthcare professionals to educate patients about the importance of preventive care, healthy lifestyle choices, and early detection of health problems.

24/7 Emergency Services

Immediate medical care for patients experiencing sudden illness or injury, including emergency room (ER) services, trauma care, and ambulance services.

Maternity and Obstetrics

Prenatal care, childbirth services, maternity wards, labor and delivery rooms, postpartum care, and neonatal intensive care units (NICUs) for newborns.

Home Health Care

In-home medical care, nursing services, and therapy services for patients who require assistance with activities of daily living or post-hospitalization care.

Wellness and Health Promotion

Health education programs, wellness screenings, preventive health services, smoking cessation programs, weight management programs, and community outreach initiatives to promote healthy living.

Nutrition and Dietary Counseling

Nutrition assessment, diet planning, and counseling services provided by registered dietitians to promote healthy eating habits and manage chronic conditions.
